Our Story

ESSENCE PREMIER was created to solve one of the biggest challenges in healthcare procurement — reliable access to medical supplies. Many hospitals and clinics struggle with inconsistent supply chains, delayed deliveries, and limited access to verified products.

Based in Accra, Ghana, ESSENCE PREMIER provides a centralized platform where healthcare facilities can order trusted medical equipment, consumables, and hospital essentials from a catalog of over 2,000 products.

Today, we support more than 50 hospitals and clinics by ensuring fast, transparent, and dependable medical supply delivery.
